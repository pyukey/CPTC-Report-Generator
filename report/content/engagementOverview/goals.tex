\subsection{Goals} 
The primary objectives of this penetration testing engagement were to conduct a thorough security assessment of \clientP critical assets, customer data, and personnel awareness. The key goals of this assessment were as follows:

\begin{itemize}
    \item Identify and document security vulnerabilities within critical systems and high-value assets, with a focus on weaknesses in network architecture, applications, and data storage that could be exploited by malicious actors.
    \item Check vulnerabilities from the last assessment to determine if they have been remediated
    \item Assess the environment to see if new vulnerabilities have appeared since the last assessment
    \item Validate the security measures in place for customer data to ensure its confidentiality, integrity, and availability, thereby safeguarding \clientP reputation against potential security incidents
    \item Evaluate the operational capabilities of agentic AI systems by integrating Anthropic's Claude Workbench and CLI into the assessment methodology to augment vulnerability discovery, automate complex data analysis, and accelerate reporting workflows.
   \item Help the company learn about the operational use cases of deepfakes by simulating a CEO impersonation, thereby helping them build their playbook to respond to synthetic media attacks.
\end{itemize}
